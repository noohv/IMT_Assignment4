This is a great book about mindmapping - a way to unleash your creativity and use the both sides of your brain. The author suggests that this can be achieved by drawing mindmaps - visual maps of the problem or topic that you want to explore. I have used mindmaps for quite some time and they have been really helpful in both my work and studies.
